# The AI Visibility Playbook

**Get your content cited by ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini.**

Only **23% of well-ranking content gets cited by AI assistants**. The other 77% is invisible in AI search — despite ranking well on Google. Google ranking and AI citation are governed by different rules, and this playbook documents those rules: a 23-element framework based on a 90-day study of 439 articles across 11 industries, tested against four major AI platforms.

## ⚡ Quick start — your first 60 seconds

The fastest path from zero to your first AI citation:

1. **Rewrite your opening 100 words.** Put the direct answer first. Zero links. This alone gets most people their first citation. → [Template](templates/direct-answer-template.md)
2. **Add "Last updated" to the top.** Make a real content update, then display the date prominently. AI rewards freshness.
3. **Convert prose data to tables.** Any comparison, pricing, or list data — put it in a table. Tables increase citation rate by 40%.
4. **Add schema markup.** Copy the Article JSON-LD into your page head. → [Snippets](snippets/)

A 2-person B2B SaaS team went from 0% to 28% citation rate in 90 days using only the four Critical Elements. Start there.

## The core insight

Traditional SEO optimizes for clicks. GEO optimizes for **trustworthiness and chunk extraction**. AI assistants extract 100–300 word chunks from qualified sources — your content must first pass source qualification, and after that, chunk clarity determines who gets cited. These require fundamentally different strategies. → [How AI search actually works](docs/how-ai-search-works.md)
