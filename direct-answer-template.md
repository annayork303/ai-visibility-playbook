# Direct Answer Template (Element 2)

Your opening 100 words determine whether AI cites you. Fill in the blanks, keep it to 90–110 words, self-contained, with **zero hyperlinks anywhere in the block**.

## The template

> **[TERM]** is **[CONCISE DEFINITION in one sentence]**. Unlike **[ALTERNATIVE]**, [TERM] **[KEY DIFFERENTIATOR]**. This matters because **[STATISTIC or QUANTIFIED IMPACT]**. **[One sentence preview of what the full article covers below.]**

## The structure behind it

| Part | Length | What to write |
|---|---|---|
| Direct Answer | 20–30 words | State the answer clearly in 1–2 sentences. Be specific, not vague. |
| Essential Context | 40–50 words | Key facts, statistics, or qualifications that support the answer. |
| Preview | 20–30 words | Briefly mention what the article covers in detail below. |

## Rules

1. **Zero links** in the block — 91% of cited answer blocks contain none. Links signal the authority lives elsewhere.
2. **90–110 words total** — over 150 words exceeds the optimal chunk extraction window.
3. **Answer first, story second** — long intros push the answer chunk out of the first 100 words.
4. **Specific over vague** — exact numbers, named sources, real dates.
